About the Breakout Game

The AI is playing the classic arcade game Breakout:

  • Basic Controls: Player controls a paddle at the bottom of the screen
  • Game Mechanics: Ball bounces around the screen, breaking bricks at the top
  • Objective: Clear all bricks while preventing the ball from falling below the paddle
  • Lives System: Player starts with multiple lives (typically 5), losing one each time the ball falls below the paddle
  • Advanced Strategy: Skilled players try to create a "tunnel" on one side to get the ball behind the brick wall for maximum efficiency

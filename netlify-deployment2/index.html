AI Analysis Comparison Study

Welcome! Thank you for participating in our research study about understanding AI behavior.

This study compares different AI analysis methods to see how effectively they help you understand a game-playing AI.

New Two-Part Study Design

This study now consists of two parts designed to comprehensively evaluate different AI analysis methods:

Part 1: Short Clip Analysis (~10 minutes)

You'll analyze short video clips using three different methods: image-based analysis, video-based analysis, and Grad-CAM visualization. For each clip, you'll rank the methods in order of preference and evaluate their effectiveness.

Part 2: Comprehensive Environment Analysis (~10 minutes)

You'll analyze five different 30-second clips from various environments, evaluating image analysis, video analysis, and highlight methods. You'll rate each method's usefulness for understanding the AI's behavior in different contexts.
